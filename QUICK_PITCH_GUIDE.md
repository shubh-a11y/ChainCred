## Q&A Prep

### Q: "Why blockchain instead of a database?"
**A:** "Immutability and decentralization. Databases can be hacked, edited, or shut down. Blockchain provides cryptographic proof that's permanent and verifiable by anyone."

### Q: "Why Stellar?"
**A:** "Three reasons: transaction costs under $0.00001, 3-5 second finality, and growing enterprise adoption. Perfect for credentials that need to be accessible and affordable globally."

### Q: "How do you prevent spam?"
**A:** "Small transaction fees naturally limit spam, and verifier requirements ensure only legitimate achievements get verified. We can also implement reputation systems."

### Q: "What about privacy?"
**A:** "We store links to evidence, not documents themselves. Users control what to share. We can implement zero-knowledge proofs for selective disclosure in future versions."

### Q: "Who are your competitors?"
**A:** "Traditional background check services like HireRight ($100+/check), centralized platforms like Credly, and emerging blockchain solutions. Our edge is Stellar's cost and speed, plus true user ownership."

### Q: "What's your business model?"
**A:** "Multiple streams: transaction fees, premium features (custom badges, analytics), enterprise plans (white-label solutions), and marketplace fees for verified credentials."

### Q: "What's the market size?"
**A:** "Professional certification market is $50B annually, corporate training is $370B, plus education credentials, freelance verification, and open-source portfolios. Massive opportunity."

### Q: "What's next?"
**A:** "We're ready for mainnet. Need partnerships with universities and certification bodies as initial verifiers. Building towards mobile app and IPFS integration for decentralized evidence storage."
